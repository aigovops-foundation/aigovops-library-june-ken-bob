The spine — every process serves one arc

Whatever the discipline, the work bends toward the same three acts. Nothing is busywork; each step moves a gate.

ACT I

Get to Yes

Discover the gates, do the work, sign the first receipt.

ACT II

Stay at Yes

Monitor, re-attest, keep the evidence fresh.

ACT III

Recover to Yes

On drift or incident: remediate, re-sign, learn.

ALWAYS

In your language

Human and agent speak the member's tongue; the receipt stays canonical.

The cast — the library staff

Each agent is a role with a narrow, reversible capability and a clear human it answers to. They are the staff; members are the citizens.

Concierge
FRONT DESK · intake & routing

Greets in-language, understands intent, routes to the right room. Never acts irreversibly.

Lantern
READING ROOM · framework mapping

Maps a problem to the frameworks and compiles the gates you'll face.

Beacon
ARCHIVES · evidence & signing

Signs every receipt, keeps the append-only ledger, hands auditors a verifiable bundle.

Maker
MAKERSPACE · design & build

Assembles workflows and agents on the canvas; previews cost and safety before anything ships.

Scribe
writes docs & help

Turns shipped work into documentation and user assistance, in plain language.

Cloud-Mary
runs the tests

Unit, e2e, scale, chaos — green before anything merges.

Polyglot
translation & i18n

Gives instant machine-translation coverage; routes human review; signs the locale bundle.

Aperture
accessibility steward

Audits every surface to WCAG 2.2 AA; nothing ships that locks someone out.

Sentinel
monitoring & alerts

Watches health, gate failures, cap breaches; pauses and escalates to a human.

Herald
reporting

Composes status, leadership, and community updates from signed evidence.

Guardian
safety · security · privacy

Strips secrets, scans for PII, holds the threat model, blocks unsafe exposure.

Host
COFFEE SHOP · community

Makes introductions over the global tables. Agents convene; humans connect.

The repeatable processes

Every discipline follows the same shape — trigger → agent does the work → human gate → signed receipt — so the work is repeatable, auditable, and warm. Twelve playbooks; the source lives in /plan/processes.

The garden of human delight

The processes are rigorous so the experience can be warm. Agents carry the bureaucracy out of sight; what people feel is welcome, light, and belonging — in their own language, at human scale.

How it ships — private repo, shareable webpage

The plan lives in a private repository; this page is the public, secret-free face of it, served as a live GitHub Pages site in the Foundation's look.

  • Private source of truth. Agents, skills, and process playbooks live in /plan in a private repo. Secrets (keys, internal endpoints) never enter it.
  • Public face. This /docs/index.html is the share-ready page — vision, cast, skills, and processes, no secrets — published via GitHub Pages.
  • Every change is governed. A site or plan edit runs the deploy-workflow skill, passes Cloud-Mary, gets a human gate, and emits a signed release receipt.
  • Live and verifiable. The same Beacon discipline that signs evidence signs releases; the page's freshness is itself auditable.
The irreversibility boundary holds. Configs, values, and verification are prepared in advance — but the irreversible clicks (creating the repo, the first push, switching on Pages, DNS) are made by Bob and Ken, after the risk is flagged.
