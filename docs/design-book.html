II. Reference → Room

→ our oculus, god-rays & warm IBL do the emotional work.

THE CEILING ARGUES

Strahov · Admont frescoes

Painted vaults narrate humanity's progress toward wisdom.

→ our dome narrates the journey to Yes — Gap · Watch · Strong.

ONE ICONIC GESTURE

Tianjin Binhai · "the Eye"

Tiers cascade around a luminous sphere — an iris of knowledge.

→ Beacon is our Eye, the heart of the Commons.

SKYLIT & FREE TO ALL

George Peabody · 1857

A frosted skylight over iron tiers — "for the free use of all persons."

→ our always-free creed, and the Commons' light.

A WALK, LINED WITH PEOPLE

Trinity Long Room · Seattle

A processional hall past busts of great minds; a continuous spiral.

→ our journey, humans first — pioneers & the Human Library.

BIOPHILIC THIRD PLACE

Beitou Taipei · Oodi Helsinki

Hanging gardens, wood, daylight; the free civic living room.

→ our gardens & Coffee Shop — a place to linger.

SOUND & CULTURE

The hush, the page-rustle; each library expresses its place.

→ per-branch soundscape & music, ten cultures.
